üìñ Usage Guide
Authentication

    Sign Up: Create a username and password. This is stored locally on your browser.

    Login: Use your credentials to access your dashboard.

Managing Tasks

    Click the "Add Task" button in the navigation bar.

    Fill in the details (Name, Description, Due Date).

    On the Home Screen, use the slider to update progress or the checkmark to complete it instantly.

Using the AI Assistant

    Navigate to the AI Help tab.

    Type questions like "How do I learn Python?" or "I feel overwhelmed."

    The assistant uses keyword analysis to provide helpful, pre-structured advice relevant to students and professionals.

Setting Reminders

    Go to the Calendar tab.

    Click a specific Day on the calendar grid.

    Set the Hour/Minute and type your message.

    Click "Set Reminder". Keep the tab open to receive the alert.

üîí Privacy & Data Storage

TaskFlow operates 100% offline. * LocalStorage: All your data (users, tasks, notes, settings) is stored in your browser's localStorage.

    No Cloud: No data is sent to any external server.

    Security Note: Since data is stored in the browser, clearing your browser cache/data will erase your tasks and account.
